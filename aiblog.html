THE NEW GENERATION OF AI


Written by Brianna Winston

The popularity of AI has skyrocketed and changed the way of life as we know it. A decade ago, AI in movies had been depicted by human like robots that hated humanity, with plans to take over the world almost like an alien figure. Now AI is used in coding, writing, and even as a conversationist in spaces like Snapchat. But the daunting question is, how far will it go and to what extent will it be used? This is a question that could send most tech geniuses spiraling.
